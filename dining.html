Table Setting:
Familiarize yourself with basic table settings, including utensil placement and napkin use.
Ordering:
  • Review the menu thoroughly before ordering.
  • Communicate any dietary restrictions or special requests to the server.
    • Dining Etiquette:
  • Wait for everyone to be served before starting the meal.
  • Use utensils from the outside in, progressing with each course.
    • Table Conversations:
  • Engage in polite and considerate conversation.
  • Keep phone usage to a minimum, especially during formal or business dining.
    • Tipping:
  • Leave a tip for the server, typically around 15-20% of the bill amount.
    • Casual vs. Formal Dining:
  • Understand the difference between casual and formal dining settings.
    • Paying the Bill:
  • The person who extended the invitation is typically expected to pay.
  • Offer to split or cover the tip as a gesture of courtesy.
